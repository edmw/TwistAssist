# Privacy Policy

TwistAssist respects your privacy.

## Data Collection

TwistAssist does not collect, track, or share any personal data.

No user accounts, application analytics, advertising identifiers, or third-party tracking tools are used.

## Solve Data

All solve results and session data are stored:

  * locally on your device, and
  * optionally synchronized via iCloud, if you enable iCloud sync.

When iCloud sync is enabled, your data is stored securely in your private iCloud container and is accessible only to you and your Apple ID. TwistAssist has no access to this data.

## Third Parties

TwistAssist does not share data with third parties and does not use external analytics or tracking services.

## Your Control

You remain in full control of your data.

All stored data can be deleted at any time by removing the app or disabling iCloud sync.

## Contact

If you have any questions about privacy, please use the support page to contact us.
